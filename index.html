It is our privilege to invite you to The LNMIIT Model United Nations 2018 scheduled to take place on 6th-7th January, 2018 at The LNM Institute of Information Technology, Jaipur.

One of the oldest and most prestigious MUNs in Jaipur, LNMIIT MUN 2018 aims to uphold its commitment to delegate experience and quality of debate.

Through this event, we seek to provide those members of the community, who are passionate about global issues, a platform that realistically simulates the proceedings that take place in the United Nations and its associated agencies. The effort has been to help the young delegates nurture their intellectual prowess, so that they may be empowered to effectively contribute to the global community.
